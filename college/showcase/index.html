Educational Code by Russell Steadman
I started to learn how to code on my own at age 12, and have gotten better over the years with practice. Here are some of the cocurricular educational websites that I have created.
Speed Quiz
I made this website to study for speed quizzes in Pre-Calculus. It gives a trigonometric function for a location on the unit circle and then evaluates your answer.
Visit
Bread & Brie
This is an website which I made for AP European History that shows rewritten lyrics to the song "Mamma Mia" by ABBA in real time with a karaoke version of the song. The new lyrics are about the French Revolution.
Visit
Choose Your Argument
This is a choose your own story website that was made with peers for an AP English Lang & Comp project.
Visit
Greek To Me
This is a tool that converts Ancient Polytonic Greek input into an alphabetized dictionary list. I made this for my dad, who has his doctorate in classical studies.
Visit
AP Euro Pro
This is a website that is capable of giving quizzes, showing useful videos, and generating timelines about European History.
